🔍 Keyword Research

← Back to Elimuhub Main Toolkit Home

Keyword research helps you discover what people in Kenya are searching for — so you can tailor your tutoring services, blog posts, and social media to match real student and parent needs.

🎯 Why Keyword Research Matters for Elimuhub

  • Helps you appear when parents search for tutors (e.g., “IGCSE math tutor in Nairobi”).
  • Shows you which subjects or locations are most in demand.
  • Guides your blog and ad campaigns for better visibility and conversion.

🧰 Free Tools to Use

  • Google Keyword Planner: Free tool inside Google Ads that shows monthly search volumes and related ideas.
  • Ahrefs Keyword Generator: Great for checking competition and keyword difficulty.
  • Semrush Keyword Magic Tool: Find keyword clusters and question-based keywords like “how to prepare for KCSE.”
  • Bing Webmaster Tools: Useful for tracking keyword performance on Bing and Yahoo searches.

💡 Elimuhub Keyword Examples

Try building content or ad campaigns around these keyword ideas:

  • home tutors in Nairobi
  • IGCSE Chemistry tutor Kenya
  • CBC Grade 7 English revision
  • KCSE Physics past papers help
  • private tuition near South C Nairobi

🚀 Pro Tip

Use long-tail keywords like “affordable IGCSE tutors in Karen Nairobi”. These are less competitive and more likely to attract serious clients looking for exactly what Elimuhub offers.
